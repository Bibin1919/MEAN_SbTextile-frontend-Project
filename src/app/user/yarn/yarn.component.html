YARN PRODUCTS

SB Textile Mills Ltd is well known for its superior quality yarns. Our yarn range stands tall in terms of both quality and durability.

Technology

  • We use the latest machines from LMW, Rieter and Trutzschler.
  • Ring spun - Carded and Combed, Compact and Siro Compact ( Eli twist), Vortex Yarn, TFO Yarn, Open End, Air jet and Slub.

YARN COUNT

  • OE - Ne 6's to 30's
  • RY - Ne 10s to 120s
  • DY - Ne 6/2 to 120/2s
  • Vortex - Ne 14s- Ne 60s
  • Melange RY - Ne 20s - Ne 50s
  • Melange OE - Ne 8s - Ne 30s

98% of the production is exported either as direct export or value added fabrics to various countries across the globe.
