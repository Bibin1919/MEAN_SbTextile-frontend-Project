COTTON FABRIC PRODUCTS

We specialize in offering superior quality woven and knitted fabrics in greige and dyed form.

Woven Fabrics: Greige and Finished

    The variety of fabric weave includes Plain, Twill, Drill, Gabardine, Sateen, Satin, Stripe Sateen Dobby design, Rib stop, Herringbone and Oxford weave and equipped with the most modern Air-jet looms. SB Group produces fabrics in widths ranging from 50 inches to 134 inches with 450 Air-Jet looms with the production of 200,000 meters/day.

  • 100% POLYESTER**

  • Elastane Products in above compositions
  • 100% VISCOSE
  • * Cotton offered in Conventional, BCI, organic, PIMA, GIZA, Australian, ** Polyester in both regular and recycled

98% of the production is exported either as direct export or value added fabrics to various countries across the globe.
